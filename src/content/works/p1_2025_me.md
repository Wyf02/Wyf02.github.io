## Overview

This is my personal portfolio and blog website, designed to showcase my work, research interests, and creative endeavors. The site serves as a digital home where I present my professional background, academic achievements, and ongoing projects.

## Features

### Portfolio Section
- **Research Showcase**: Detailed descriptions of my publications and research projects
- **Project Gallery**: Visual representations of selected work
- **Interactive Elements**: Hover effects, smooth transitions, and responsive design

### About Me
A comprehensive introduction including:
- Educational background (Fudan University, HKU)
- Research interests in AI, visualization, and computational social science
- Personal interests beyond academia

### Technical Stack
- **Frontend**: Vue.js 3, HTML/CSS/JavaScript
- **Design**: Custom styling with dark theme aesthetic
- **Build Tool**: Vue CLI
- **Deployment**: GitHub Pages

## Design Philosophy

The site reflects my belief that personal branding should:
1. Be authentic and human-centered
2. Balance professional polish with personal touch
3. Showcase not just achievements but also values and interests
4. Be accessible and user-friendly

## Color Palette

The design uses a carefully curated color scheme:
- Primary: Sage green (#5BA689)
- Accent: Ocean blue (#4DA3FF)
- Neutrals: Dark backgrounds with light text for accessibility

## Development Process

The site has undergone several iterations:
1. Initial MVP focusing on about and works sections
2. Adding responsive design for mobile devices
3. Integrating dynamic routing for work details
4. Implementing markdown-based content management
